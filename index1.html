About Jumpfrika

This Unique and versatile brand started in 2020 as Simplyjuliemedia but later rebranded as Jumpfrika in June 2021 making it a total of 2 years business existence. We started solely as a photography (Nature/landscape) brand and was producing Picture frames and photography prints. But today we offer other important services to clients and we are 100% effective in delivering our duties. At Jumpfrika we help you capture and create beautiful moments, we help you plan field trips, Excursions, vacation and explore the immediate surroundings. We also give our best as we offer language tutorials and translations for travel and study purposes. We do Creative Writing & Brand mangement. Partner with us as we journey through the lens and get creativity at its peak.
